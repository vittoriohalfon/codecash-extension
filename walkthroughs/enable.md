# Track earnings & stay in control

Once connected, codecash turns on automatically. Here's what's happening and how to stay in control.

While enabled, codecash:

- Backs up your current `~/.claude/settings.json` (restored exactly on disable).
- Sets the Claude Code spinner verb + status line to render the current ad as a clickable
  terminal link.
- Accrues on-screen view time and credits your account once an ad has been visible long enough.

**Stay in control:**

- The `codecash` item in the status bar shows today's earnings, or **codecash — paused** when ads are off.
- **codecash: Disable ad injection** restores your original settings exactly, any time.
- It never throws into your CLI — every settings edit is reversible and every spawned child is
  time-bounded.
