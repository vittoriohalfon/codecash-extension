# Connect & start earning

codecash shows **one** tasteful sponsored ad in the wait states of your AI coding agent
(the Claude Code spinner + status line) and pays you a revenue share for it.

Click **Connect & start earning** and we'll:

1. Open the codecash sign-in page in your browser.
2. Mint a device token for this editor and hand it straight back — no copy-paste.
3. Turn on ad injection automatically and start serving — you're earning, no command palette needed.

Your current `~/.claude/settings.json` is backed up first and restored exactly whenever you disable.

Already have a token? Run **codecash: Sign in with a token (paste)** from the Command Palette
and paste it instead.
